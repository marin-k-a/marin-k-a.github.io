Voyage

Our journeys and special moments.

  • December 21, 2011

    Our first conversation

    We were working together on NIC.UA’s New Year’s promo, and our first chat was in Adium:
    — Hi! :)
    — hi :)
    — My name is Alex, I'm Psyho designer. I'm working on stories layout now.

  • December 24, 2011

    The day we first met

    Our companies decided to make New Year’s party together in Dnipropetrovsk, Ukraine.
    And there we first met.

  • February 11, 2012

    First unofficial date

    Alex came to Kyiv and offered to have a breakfast in the morning, but I slept through it successfully. Nevertheless, we met in the evening and had tea at Mafia restaurant on Khreshchatyk. A year later on the same day we got married :)

  • February 14, 2012

    Valentine card

    Alex sent me a digital Valentine card with a very interesting quest. I still remember it.

  • March 9, 2012

    Odessa

    We had a very awkward meeting in Odessa. Alex presented me a selfmade doormat, and I brought him condensed milk (sgushchenka) from Belarus.

  • April 6, 2012

    The first kiss

    The week earlier I suggested to go somewhere for a day, and we bought tickets to the Crimea on April 6. On that day In Feodosia on the beach we had our first kiss. It was very romantic :)

  • April – May, 2012

    Our first long trip. We went to Chernivtsy through Kamenets-Podil’skiy, and after went to Kyiv on a bus. Chernovtsy is a very beautiful city! In Kyiv we watch ‘Into The Wild’ on iPad in the park.

  • May 16, 2012

    The first borscht!

    In Dnipropetrovsk I made my first borscht. I still think that it was the reason why we married :)

  • June, 2012

    In June we have visited each other in Kyiv and Dnipropetrovsk, went to the Kyiv sea, and had two nice one day trips — in Poltava and Sudak.

  • July, 2012

    We were driving from Kyiv to Odessa to the Yandex conference. After we took the train to Fiolent (Crimea) to celebrate my birthday. It was the greatest birthday ever!

  • July – August, 2012

    Alex went to Germany to work on project with Felix and Felix. He was in Saarbrücken, Riegelsberg, Cologne and had a day trip to Strasbourg (France). I was very happy for him :)

  • August, 2012

    In this month we were in Kharkiv and met with my friend Kirill, and had a great trip together with Alina and Zaur to Vinnitsa and Uman.
    Brushchatka forever!

  • September 1, 2012

    Our engagement

    Alex surprised me. He arrived by bus from Dnipropetrovsk to Kyiv, and after I washed the dishes, he proposed to me :) And I said ‘Yes’!

  • September, 2012

    We moved in

    At the end of the month I’ve moved from Kyiv to Dnipropetrovsk and we moved in together in our first apartment on Bohdana Khmelnitskogo str.
